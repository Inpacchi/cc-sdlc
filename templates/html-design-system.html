15 SVG Diagrams

Conventions: 1.5px neutral strokes, 2px emphasized. All rects use rx=10. Labels: 13px sans inside nodes, 11px mono for code/IDs, 12px sans gray-500 for annotations. Semantic colors: Clay=focus, Olive=success, Danger=error, Info=external. Solid=synchronous, dashed=asynchronous.
